FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your consultation.

Is a hair transplant painful?
No. The procedure is performed under local anaesthesia. Most patients feel little to no discomfort. Post-procedure mild soreness resolves in 2–3 days.
How long does the procedure take?
FUE hair transplant takes 6–8 hours depending on graft count. It is completed in a single day and you can go home the same evening.
When will I see results?
New growth begins in 3–4 months. Significant improvement is visible at 6 months, and full natural results at 12 months. Results are permanent.
What is the recovery time?
Most patients return to desk work in 2–3 days. Avoid strenuous activity for 2 weeks. Hair looks completely natural within 12 months.
How much does it cost?
Cost depends on the number of grafts required, which varies per patient. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Book a free consultation for your personalised quote.
Are the results permanent?
Yes. Transplanted follicles come from a donor zone genetically resistant to hair loss — making results permanent for life.
